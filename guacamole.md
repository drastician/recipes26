# Guacamole Recipe

## Ingredients:
- 2 avocados
- 1 lime
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 jalapeño
- 1/2 onion
- 1 clove garlic
- dash of cumin
- scrunch of black pepper
 
## Instructions:
- cut and mash avocados
- chop onion and jalapeño
- grate the garlic in
- squeeze lime
- add salt, cumin, and bp
- and mix well
